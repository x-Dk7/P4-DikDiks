Lets Learn about Mario Sunshine

Super Mario Sunshine released on July 12th, 2002. If you love beach resorts, this game is just for you! Follow Mario in a 3D environment as he explores the beaches of Delfino Plaza! This game comes with a twist however! Mario can use a new machine, known as FLUDD in order to shoot himslef up in the air with water! Your quest is to save Princess Peach from Bowser's son who believes that Peach is his own mother! While exploring the island, Mario can collect 121 Power Stars to explore new areas! Use the Gamecube controller to allow Mario to shoot up in the sky using FLUDD! You'll meet plenty of familiar and new faces, like Toad and Luigi! Check out the game now!
